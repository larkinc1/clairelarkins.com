Nutritional Science Student

Hi, I’m Claire! I’m a nutritional science student living in San Francisco. I use my creativity to find solutions to problems in healthcare. My focus is to prevent and reverse diet-related chronic diseases by increasing education for early detection and improving disease management through integrative care.

About Me

I grew up on a farm in rural Oregon, and I’ve seen from personal experience how people struggle with poor access to food, lack of culinary skills, and misinformation about nutrition. I want to support my community by improving nutrition security. I have strong professional experience and volunteer work at nonprofit organizations with community-oriented missions that inspire me. I recently returned to school to become a registered dietitian to further my impact.

I always seek integrative health, culinary medicine, public health, and sustainability projects. Feel free to reach out, and together we can work to improve the health of our community.

Experience

Farms to Grow - Urban Garden Volunteer, Oakland, CA

Collaborated in teams to plant, grow and harvest produce using sustainable practices like no-till farming and crop covering. I maintained a variety of edible crops and prepared compost.

WWOOF - Farmhand, Naxos, Greece

Trained in regenerative agriculture, permaculture, seed saving, and traditional Greek cooking. I planted seeds, tended to starters, and harvested wild herbs.

Sprouts - Volunteer, Nonprofit Chef Training Program, San Francisco, CA

Fundraised tuition for chef training for disadvantaged youths at Sprout’s annual gala. I contributed to a live auction and panel event with Alice Waters, Michael Pollan, and Dominique Crenn.

18 Reasons: Cooking Matters - Volunteer, Nonprofit Cooking School, San Francisco, CA

Delivered cooking education and meal planning lessons at Saint Mary's Medical Center under a registered dietitian. I taught knife skills, food preparation, and nutrition with an emphasis on diabetes management.

San Francisco SPCA - Veterinarian Technician, San Francisco, CA

Delivered medical care to animals at the SPCA Hospital. I performed venipuncture, vaccinations, metabolic panels, cultures, and microscopy. I acted as a liaison between veterinarians and clients.

Cellar 55 - Sommelier’s Assistant and Tasting Room Manager, Vancouver, WA

Ran operations at a wine storage facility and tasting room. I designed charcuterie and wine tasting menus, catered events and hired monthly artists and musicians. I learned about viticulture and local business ownership under a sommelier.

Habitat for Humanity - Americorps Site Lead, Vancouver, WA

Coordinated teams of volunteers of up to thirty people to build houses. I taught carpentry, tool use, and team-building skills. I co-hosted events like Women’s Day, Youth Builds, and All Faith Days to encourage diversity and inclusivity on the construction site.

American Red Cross - Lead Phlebotomist, Portland, OR

On-site supervisor for blood drives across the Pacific Northwest. I trained staff, managed events, and performed medical screenings and blood collections for partnering hospitals. I received my license in Medical Assisting, Phlebotomy, and First Aid.

Health Care in the Himalayas- Intern, India and Nepal

Fieldwork program with a pre-med emphasis. I trained in patient intake for emergency pop-up clinics, calculated nutritional assessments, prepared meals for around 100 kids, and collected data on social determinants of health (ACEs).
