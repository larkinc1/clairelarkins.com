About Me

I grew up in rural Oregon and Washington, and I’ve seen from personal experience how people suffer from poor access to food, lack of culinary skills, and misinformation about nutrition. I want to support my community to feel better by eating better. I have a strong history of professional experience and volunteering work at nonprofit organizations working to improve public health. I pursue organizations with community-oriented missions that inspire me. I recently returned to school to become a Registered Dietician to further my impact.

I am always seeking opportunities to dive in to projects in integrative health, public health, culinary medicine, agriculture, and sustainability. Feel free to reach out and together we can work to improve wellness in our community.

Experience

WWOOF - Volunteer, Farmhand, Naxos, Greece

As a farmhand, I learned about biodynamic agriculture, permaculture, seed saving, medicinal plants, and traditional Greek cooking. I planted vegetables, laid compost, and harvested wild herbs.

Sprouts - Volunteer, Nonprofit Chef Training Program, San Francisco

Fundraised tuition for chef training for disadvantaged youths at Sprout’s annual gala. I assisted with a dinner, live auction, and panel event with Alice Waters, Michael Pollan and Dominique Crenn.

18 Reasons - Volunteer, Nonprofit Cooking School, San Francisco

Assisted in teaching cooking classes at Saint Mary's Medical Center under a Registered Dietician. Taught knife skills, food preparation, meal planning, and nutrition with an emphasis on diabetes management.

San Francisco SPCA - Veterinarian Assistant, San Francisco

Delivered medical care to animals at the SPCA Hospital. Performed venipuncture, metabolic panels, microscopy, and vaccinations, with an emphasis on low-stress handling. Acted as liaison between veterinarians and clients.

San Francisco Food Bank - Volunteer, Marin County

Contributed to community food drives, assembling vegetables and fruits in take home packages. Learned about the local networks of food production.

Wine Cellar 55 - Sommelier’s Assistant and Tasting Room Manager , Vancouver, WA

Ran operations at a wine storage facility and tasting room. Designed charcuterie board and wine tasting menus, catered events, and managed online presence. Hired monthly artists and musicians for the tasting room. Learned about tasting, viticulture and local business ownership under a Sommelier.

Habitat for Humanity - Construction Site Lead, Vancouver, WA

Coordinated teams of volunteers of up to thirty people to work together to build houses. Taught carpentry, tool use, and housing repair skills. Hosted events like Women’s Day build, Youth build, and All Faith days to encourage diversity and inclusivity on the construction site.

American Red Cross - Lead Phlebotomist, Portland, Oregon

Acted as lead phlebotomist and on-site supervisor for blood drives across the pacific northwest. Trained staff, managed events, and processed blood donations for partnering hospitals. Received my licenses in Medical Assisting, Phlebotomy and First Aid to perform medical screenings and blood collection.

Health Care in the Himalayas - Intern, India and Nepal

Field work program with a pre-med emphasis. I medically assisted in emergency pop-up clinics, performed nutritional assessments, prepared meals for around 100 kids, and collected data on social determinants of health (ACE’s).
